SpagBol

Description

Ingredients

  • Tin of tomatoes
  • 200g mince per serving
  • 1 clove garlic per serving
  • 1 fresh chili per serving
  • salt
  • black pepper
  • cumin
  • paprika
  • spaghetti
  • olive oil
  • tomato paste

Steps

  1. turn pan on medium high heat with olive oil
  2. chop garlic and chili and add to pan
  3. cook until fragrant
  4. add mince
  5. mash up mince while adding pepper until meat is browned
  6. add spaghetti to pot of boiling water
  7. add salt, and spices
  8. add tomato paste
  9. add tin of tomatoes
  10. cook until flavours have gotten to know eachother
  11. taste and season as needed
  12. dish
